    WATER PROOFING AND DAMP PROOFING
    We use the latest technology on the market to control damp and keep the walls dry. We believe in quality hence we use the only the best products on all our damp and Waterproofing projects. Our preferred materials are Dampcrete, a cement based Waterproofing Solution applied to the brickwork to prevent damp or water penetrating to the rest of the building. Then Coprox also a cement based Waterproofing Solutions, effective and efficient to most building works, applied in your plaster mixture to prevent further moisture on affected surface areas.
